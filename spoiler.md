### Event Descriptions

* **What that squeak?**: Randomly spawns multiple landmines near outdoor players.
* **Don't move!**: Spawns several turrets in the player's immediate vicinity.
* **Size matters!**: Randomly shrinks or grows a player, scaling their physical hitbox and shifting their voice pitch.
* **Turrets got rabies!**: Forces all active turrets into a 360-degree berserk firing mode for a set duration.
* **Football**: All lootable items become kickable footballs with realistic bouncing physics.
* **Adrenaline**: Grants infinite stamina but forces the player to run forward constantly.
* **Hunting**: Spawns a random monster (indoor or outdoor) near a living player.
* **Where am i?**: Teleports all indoor players to random AI nodes within the facility.
* **Lunch Break!**: Suddenly teleports one random player from the facility back to the ship.
* **Something's Watching**: Plays terrifying, random enemy audio clips near players inside the facility.
* **Firefly (Passive)**: Picking up the Apparatus causes the player to glow with a permanent point light for the rest of the round.
* **Positioning error**: Instantly swaps the positions and states of two random eligible players.
* **Company Message**: Displays fake high-priority system warnings (like "Ship Leaving") on everyone's HUD.
